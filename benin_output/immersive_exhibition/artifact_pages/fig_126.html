Ears badly formed. Coral choker, badge of rank. Guilloche pattern, with pellets round neck. The face is very much tooled and filed, and the lips and nose flattened by Crotals have probably been susjjended from the eyelets below, as filing. indicated by the eight links of chains left remaining (see Plate XIX, Fig. 112).
