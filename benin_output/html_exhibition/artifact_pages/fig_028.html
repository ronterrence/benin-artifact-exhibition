Ivory carved tusk, 4 feet 1 inch long from bottom to point ; The other ornamentation traversed by five bands of interlaced strap-work. consists of :-Human figures with hands crossed on breast ; bird standing on pedestal ; human figures with hands holding sashes ; trees growing down- wards ; a rosette ; mudfish ; crocodiles with heads upwards ; a serpent with sinuous body, head downwards ; two cups ; a serjaent, head upwards ; detached human heads. Some of the representations are so rude that it requires experience to understand their meaning. On this tusk the inter- laced pattern is the prevailing ornament, and it passes into the guilloche This tusk is more tastefully decorated than the other tusk, pattern.
