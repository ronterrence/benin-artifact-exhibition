At top a bottle bound with Small attendant with paddle in right hand. grass, and figure of some object, perhaps a stone celt bound with grass. copper nails. Brass and iron screws are used for ornamentation in this carving. Guilloche pattern of two bands without pellets around the edge of the panel.
